Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Aura Ceylon collects and uses your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, delivery address, city, and order details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, deliver products, respond to customer inquiries, provide order updates, and improve our service.

3. Payment Information

Aura Ceylon does not store sensitive card details directly on this website unless a secure payment system is added in the future. For now, payment methods such as cash on delivery or bank transfer may be used.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share necessary details only with delivery services or partners when required to complete your order.

5. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

This website may use browser storage or similar technologies to remember your cart, improve your shopping experience, and support app-like features.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to request updates or deletion of the personal information you have shared with us, subject to legal or operational requirements.

8. Changes to This Policy

Aura Ceylon may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact Aura Ceylon through the contact page or by email at info@auraceylon.com.
